About the Founder

 James Bach, is a highly skilled systems engineer and software developer with over 10 years of experience managing and developing complex communications systems in the defense industry. As a Lead Systems Engineer/Technical Director he has led multidisciplinary teams, streamlined integration processes, and provided critical technical support for multimillion dollar programs. James' expertise in program management and techincal background in computer engineering provides the expertise needed to successfully launch Restoration Twelve.

 Beyond engineering, James has personally experienced the challenges of mental health and substance abuse struggles all while growing toward spiritual healing. Through seasons of difficulty and growth, he developed a deep passion for combining faith and technology to help others find hope and restoration. This personal journey fuels his vision for Restoration Twelve. His mission is to bring faith and innovation together to help others walk the path of healing and freedom.
