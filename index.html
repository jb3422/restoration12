About Restoration Twelve

Restoration Twelve is a faith-based recovery platform created to help individuals find hope, healing, and community support in their journey toward sobriety. Our mission is to blend proven recovery principles with the power of faith and spiritual encouragement.

The app offers daily reflections tied to scripture, tools to track sobriety milestones, and opportunities to connect with sponsors and Christian recovery groups both locally and online.

Restoration Twelve was founded by James Bach, an engineer with over a decade of experience designing and managing advanced communications systems in the defense industry. Beyond his professional career, James has walked through seasons of personal challenges, including mental health struggles and substance abuse, and discovered profound spiritual transformation. His passion is to combine technology and faith to help others navigate the path to recovery with hope and support.

Whether you‚Äôre beginning your journey or have been walking it for years, Restoration Twelve is here to remind you that with faith, restoration is possible.
